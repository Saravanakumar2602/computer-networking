# Critical Protocols & Concepts

## ARP (Address Resolution Protocol)
- Maps IP addresses to MAC addresses (Layer 2).
- Command: `arp -a`

## ICMP (Internet Control Message Protocol)
- Used for diagnostics (ping, traceroute).
- Messages: Echo Request/Reply, Destination Unreachable, etc.

## OSI vs TCP/IP Model
- OSI: 7 layers
- TCP/IP: 4 layers

## IPv6 Transition Mechanisms
- Dual-Stack, Tunneling, NAT64

## CIDR (Classless Inter-Domain Routing)
- Flexible subnetting (e.g., 192.168.0.0/24)
